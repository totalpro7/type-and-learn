About this website

This is a free WPM test and typing speed test. You can measure typing speed (WPM/CPM) and accuracy. Unlike many typing tests, it uses interesting learning texts (science, fun facts, general knowledge) instead of random words.

How is WPM calculated?

We use a common standard: (correct characters ÷ 5) ÷ minutes. The “5” assumes an average word length of five characters.

Why learning texts?

Many people stay more focused when the content is meaningful or interesting. That’s why you can practice with short, medium, or long texts full of facts. The goal is a double learning effect: train your fingers and learn something new.

How to use the test

  • Pick a topic (or “Mixed”).
  • Pick a text length (short, medium, long).
  • Start typing — the timer starts automatically.
  • Best scores and recent runs are saved locally in your browser.

Stats are stored in your browser (LocalStorage). No account, no signup.
